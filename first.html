Nagarjuna College of Management Studies, the recent addition to Nagarjuna Group of Institutions has been established by Nagarjuna Education Society with an objective to facilitate the rural students of Chickaballapur and Bangalore Rural districts with quality education in the fields of Commerce, Management & Science. Keeping in view that "Knowledge is Power"; NCMS is trying to reach out to every corner of these areas. The college is affiliated to Bengaluru North University & is located beside NH-44 and shares the 100-acres campus with NCET. NCMS is known for providing excellent facilities, high-quality education and it is also embedded with skill certification courses. It offers undergraduate programmes in commerce, business studies, science and postgraduate programme in commerce & management. The unique feature of education at NCMS is its strong focus on skill enhancement and holistic development of students. link to NCET website is NCET
